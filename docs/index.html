FAQs

Who can participate in the Artrox Competition? +

The competition is open to students from all countries.

How can I submit my artwork? +

Participants can submit their artwork through the official submission form available on the website. Uploaded files should be clear, high quality, and properly visible.(Sending your original artwork through courier to the Artrox VMAIL Center is mandatory for verification and authenticity purposes.)

Why do winners need to courier the original artwork? +

In today’s AI-driven world, digital images can be artificially generated, edited, or manipulated very easily. To maintain fairness, authenticity, and artistic integrity, Artrox may require selected winners to courier their original physical artwork for verification purposes.

How will the winners be selected? +

All entries will be reviewed by the Artrox jury panel based on creativity, originality, artistic quality, composition, and overall presentation.

What prizes will winners receive? +

Winners will receive cash prizes, official certificates, medals, and international recognition through the Artrox platform. (Every participant will receive an official Medal and Certificate at the address provided during the online submission process.)

About Artrox


Artrox International Drawing Competition is a global platform created to celebrate creativity, imagination, and artistic expression. The competition welcomes artists, illustrators, designers, and creative minds from around the world to showcase their unique artistic vision.Whether you work with traditional drawing, painting, digital illustration, sketching, or mixed media, Artrox provides an opportunity to present your artwork before an international audience and gain worldwide recognition.

Our mission is to encourage emerging and professional artists by creating a modern, accessible, and inspiring competition experience that values originality, creativity, and artistic excellence.Selected participants and winners will receive international exposure, official recognition, and exclusive awards including cash prizes, certificates, and medals.At Artrox, we believe that art has no boundaries — every line, color, and idea has the power to inspire the world.
